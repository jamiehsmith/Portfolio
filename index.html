Net Calories Fitbit Versa Clock Face

A work in progress, I'm making a Fitbit Versa clock face that will display a user's net calories (calories consumed minus calories burned by exercise). The app will also display the current weather in user's location, number of steps, and amount of water consumed.

Created using Fitbit Studio, Javascript, SVG and CSS.
